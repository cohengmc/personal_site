PB n J on an Everything Bagel!

Don't knock it till you try it

Peanut butter and jelly on an open face Everything is one of my favorite foods. Another alternative that slaps is a blueberry bagel toasted with peanut butter open face. The blueberries bring the sweetness like the jelly and then because its toasted, the peanut butter gets super runny. Liquid gold.
